### Survey Types I Specialize In

**Due Diligence Surveys** *(Investment research)*
Market validation surveys used by investors to assess Indonesian market opportunities, consumer behavior, brand perception, and competitive positioning ahead of acquisitions or market entry decisions. Accuracy in financial and business terminology is critical. These surveys reach Indonesian business professionals, consumers, and sector experts on behalf of global investment teams.

**Strategy Surveys** *(Management consulting)*
Corporate strategy surveys targeting Indonesian industry experts, senior executives, and business decision-makers. Designed to help management consulting teams gather primary research insights, market sizing, trend validation, competitive intelligence, from the local market. Requires a formal yet accessible register that resonates with professional respondents.

**Pricing Research Surveys** *(Consumer \& B2B research)*
Technically demanding pricing research instruments translated with precise methodological equivalency. Includes:

* **Conjoint analysis** : multi-attribute trade-off scenarios requiring carefully worded feature descriptions
* **Max diff (best-worst scaling)** : comparative preference questions where phrasing consistency across items is essential
* **Gabor-Granger** : price sensitivity ladders where each threshold must be phrased consistently

Incorrect translation in any of these question types directly undermines the validity of the pricing model. I understand the mechanics of each methodology and translate accordingly.

**Fund \& Investor Research Surveys** *(Private equity)*
Investment conviction-building surveys for fund research teams reaching Indonesian respondents. Topics covered include consumer sentiment, brand awareness, purchase behavior, market sizing, category growth, and sector-specific behavioral data. Used by private equity firms and hedge funds to validate investment theses in the Indonesian market.
